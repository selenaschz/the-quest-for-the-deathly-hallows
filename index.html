Hogwarts: The Quest for the Deathly Hallows

Story

Lord Voldemort has returned, and he already possesses the Elder Wand , one of the three Deathly Hallows and the most powerful of all wands. Now, his goal is to obtain the remaining two Hallows to consolidate his power and rule the wizarding world: Resurrection Stone will allow him to summon the dead and strengthen his army, and the Invisibility Cloak will make him undetectable while he carries out his sinister plan.

As a brave student of Hogwarts, your task is to prevent Voldemort from obtaining these relics. You must explore Hogwarts and its surroundings and face magical enemies.

Use your magical abilities to overcome the obstacles in your path. The future of the wizarding world depends on you: if you manage to acquire the relics before Voldemort, you can save the magical community from chaos. Are you ready to become the hero that the wizarding world needs?
